Sangria

Sangria! Sangria!

The best sangria recipe I've have ever tried. Great for summer parties

I recommend you use fruity Spanish wine, such as Garnacha.

Ingredients

  • 1 (750 milliliter) bottle dry red wine
  • 1/2 cup brandy
  • 1/2 cup triple sec
  • 1/3 cup frozen lemonade concentrate
  • 1/3 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup white sugar (optional)
  • 1 medium orange, sliced into rounds
  • 1 medium lemon, sliced into rounds
  • 1 medium lime, sliced into round
  • 8 maraschino cherries
  • 2 cups carbonated water (optional)

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix together red wine, brandy, triple sec, lemonade concentrate, orange juice, lemon juice, and sugar in a bowl. Add orange, lemon, and lime slices and maraschino cherries.
  3. Serve immediately over ice, or refrigerate 8 hours to overnight for best flavor. For a fizzy sangria, add club soda just before serving.
